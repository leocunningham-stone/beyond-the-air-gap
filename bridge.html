Home Works

These are not screening questions.

There are no right answers. There is only what is true for you at this moment.

Answer as plainly as you can.

Question one

When you or your organisation produces its best work — what condition makes that possible?

Question two

What is the cost of the last significant decision you didn't make?

Question three

What would it mean to be right about something that nobody around you can currently see?

These will be read. If there is something worth a conversation, you will hear back.
