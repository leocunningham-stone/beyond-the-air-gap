You are in an exposed position.

Not visibly. Not yet. The metrics look right. The board is satisfied. The AI adoption story is holding. But beneath the performance, the judgment layer is thinning — and the system you are using to monitor it is the same system producing the problem.

The Confirmation

In March 2026 Deutsche Bank asked three AI tools whether AI would solve the economy's inflation problem. The machines disagreed with the consensus. More precisely — they hedged. Flat probabilities. Cautious qualifications. The most statistically acceptable answer available. Deutsche Bank's own economists identified the mechanism: the AI, having been trained on a corpus of economists, was simply behaving like one. Two-handed. Non-committal. Safe.

A system asked a direct question about its own economic legacy could not answer it. The architecture does not permit it.

The same week, an Anthropic study confirmed that AI tools are theoretically capable of automating 94% of computer and mathematics work and 90% of office and administrative roles. The adoption gap between current deployment and that theoretical ceiling is not a comfort. It is a timer.

The Parallel

A select few identified the sub-prime exposure before the system confirmed it. The signal was present. The internal verification architecture routed it through the same lawyers, the same auditors, the same management consensus — and returned the answer it was built to return. The consequences arrived anyway.

The AI adoption cycle is not the sub-prime crisis. The mechanism is identical.

The Pattern

The organisations that will contract over the next decade are not the ones that ignored AI. They are the ones that deployed it without maintaining the one function it cannot perform — the sovereign human judgment that sees clearly before the data confirms it, holds the signal when the room overrides it, and finds the move that does not exist in any training data because it has not happened yet.

That function is thinning in direct proportion to AI adoption. The talent that carries it is leaving. The muscle that produces it is atrophying through disuse. The board is approving the next phase of investment in the system that is accelerating the loss.

The Mitigation

The Fidelity Cell is the correction.

Not a programme. Not a consultancy engagement. A protected function — outside the consensus architecture, proportionate in cost, asymmetric in return. The clean room for judgment that the semiconductor industry understood decades ago and that no organisation has yet applied to the human signal its AI depends on.

The window is not indefinite. The moment to act is before the contraction is visible, not after it is certain.

A Saturday or Sunday morning. Open ground. A conversation the environment conducts rather than a script either of us follows.

Solvitur ambulando.
